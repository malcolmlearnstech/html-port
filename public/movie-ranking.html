The Best Movies According to Malcolm

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Halloween II (1981)

A personal horror classic, steeped in a favorite memory of being about 7 or 8 years old.

No Country for Old Men (2007)

Shot really well, with simply interesting ideas/philosophies about life embedded.

A Christmas Story (1983)

Personal holiday classic, have and will continue to watch every Christmas till the end of time.
